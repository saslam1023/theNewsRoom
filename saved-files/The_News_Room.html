New black gates?
Why not blue?

By Safeeyah

You may have seen the new black gates in the playground. We were wondering why they were changed from the blue barriers and so we put this question to Ms Crandon who informed us that the barriers were temporary and were going to be replaced with the gates anyway. 

But why black? Maybe they should have been a bit brighter to match the playground. What colour gates would you have chosen? Let us know!
